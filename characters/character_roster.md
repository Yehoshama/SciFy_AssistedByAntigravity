# Character Roster

## Kaelen Vance (Main Character)
*   **Primary Profession:** Intelligence Officer (Combat/Tactical Training).
*   **Secondary Profession:** Xenobiology and Mineral Researcher.
*   **Role on Ship:** He is the ship's **Senior Military Officer**. His actual designated job is Intelligence, actively monitoring security protocols, risk assessments, and tactical data. However, because humanity has known centuries of peace, his primary duties offer significant downtime. During his idle hours, he acts as a secondary assistant to the Xenobiology department (under Lyra).
*   **Background:** Top-of-his-class academy graduate. Highly disciplined, logical, and reliant on data.

## Captain Aris Voss
*   **Primary Profession:** Command / Navigation
*   **Secondary Profession:** Psychology
*   **Tertiary Profession:** Linguist
*   **Role on Ship:** The Captain of the exploration vessel *Zenith*. 
*   **Background:** Male, relatively young for the position of Captain on a state-of-the-art vessel. This suggests he is exceptionally talented, perhaps politically connected, or a prodigy of the rationalist state. His secondary and tertiary skills make him uniquely suited for multi-generational missions, managing crew morale and communicating across diverse linguistic barriers.

## Elara
*   **Primary Profession:** Chief Engineer (Focus on Jump-Drive Tech)
*   **Secondary Profession:** Medical
*   **Role on Ship:** Responsible for the jump drive and the ship's mechanical integrity. She is likely the first to understand the severity of the malfunction, while her secondary medical background allows her to triage injuries simultaneously if the drive explodes.

## Lyra
*   **Primary Profession:** Xenobiology Researcher (Senior)
*   **Secondary Profession:** Engineering 
*   **Role on Ship:** Kaelen's research supervisor and head of the xenobiology department. 
*   **Background:** Female, very close friends with Kaelen from their time at the academy, essentially family. Her secondary skill in engineering makes her highly versatile, especially in a crisis where both biological and mechanical systems overlap.

## The Profession System:
The society operates on a rigorous system of **Primary, Secondary (and sometimes Tertiary) Professions**. 
*   **Function:** Every individual is principally occupied by their **Primary Profession**, and there is always a dedicated specialist for every role on a ship.
*   **Redundancy & Overlap:** When an individual is idle from their primary duties, they are required to assist in the department of their Secondary Profession. This ensures there are always highly trained assistants available and cross-disciplinary redundancy, meaning there is no "single point of failure" in critical systems or missions.
