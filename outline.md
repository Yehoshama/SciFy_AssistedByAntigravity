# Story Outline

## Part 1: The Vain Search
*   **The Setting:** An advanced human civilization with jump-drive technology that spans across multiple sectors. Despite their reach, space seems entirely empty of life. 
*   **The Society:** The civilization is strictly atheist and highly rational. Rejecting logic for belief, even agnosticism, is considered shameful and socially unacceptable.
*   **The Protagonist:** A top-of-his-class academy graduate serving as an Intelligence Officer. Because humanity has known peace for centuries, his combat training is largely symbolic and kept for protocol. His practical, day-to-day job is serving as a Xenobiology and Mineral Researcher, analyzing data and samples from dead space.
*   **The Mission:** The protagonist is assigned to a newly constructed, state-of-the-art exploration ship. The mission is a prestigious 3-year expedition to scan several remote, deep-space sectors for any signs of life or valuable resources.
*   **The Slump:** Fast forward 2.5 years into the mission. Several sectors have been thoroughly scanned, yielding absolute silence. The initial excitement and prestige of the mission have faded into monotony and disappointment for the crew.
*   **The Incident:** As the ship initiates a jump to their final designated sector, a critical malfunction occurs within the jump drive. 
*   **The Unknown:** The ship emerges from the jump into completely uncharted space. Navigation systems are useless, and even their best scanners fail to identify a single known star or constellation. They are entirely disconnected from known space.
