# Location and Setting Details

## The Ship: "The Zenith"
*   **Purpose:** Built for deep-space exploration, specifically designed to handle multi-generational scenarios if necessary.
*   **Capabilities:** Fully self-sustaining. Equipped with advanced manufacturing, fabrication, and comprehensive research facilities.
*   **Aesthetics/Amenities:** Unlike pure military vessels, this ship includes dedicated spaces for leisure, recreation, and long-term psychological health, recognizing the needs of a crew on a potentially endless journey.

## The Origin Point: Protos
*   **Status:** The heart of the advanced human civilization. A hyper-rational, atheistic society.
*   **Aesthetics (The Garden of Eden):** Because of their advanced technology, Protos looks like a pristine paradise. All heavy production, unsightly manufacturing, and industry have been moved entirely into planetary orbit, leaving the surface a perfectly engineered garden for human civilization.
*   **Atmosphere:** A place where logic and efficiency have triumphed over belief and mystery.

## The Unknown Sector
*   **Status:** The current location of the ship after the jump drive malfunction.
*   **Characteristics:** An unmapped asteroid field orbiting an unknown star. The sky is full of stars, but none of the familiar glowing bands of the Helios Cluster or known constellations are present. Every star visible is entirely foreign and uncharted.
